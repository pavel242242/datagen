3. ENTERPRISE UNDERPERFORMANCE: 26% churned, only 25% Day 7 activation

   - Likely needs multi-user setup, dedicated support, custom workflows

================================================================================
METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

Data Sources:
- account.parquet: 500 accounts with signup_date, tier, company_name, industry
- usage_session.parquet: 5,807 sessions with session_start, duration_minutes, actions_count
- Analysis Window: Jan 1, 2023 - Jun 29, 2024 (reference date: Dec 31, 2024 for churn status)

Definitions:
- Activation: >=1 session
- Engagement: >=5 sessions lifetime
- Day X Retention: first session within X days of signup
- Churn Status: based on days since last session (Active: 0-7, Disengaging: 7-30, etc.)
- Cohort: grouped by signup month

Tool: DuckDB (vectorized SQL queries directly on parquet files)
All metrics validated, no missing data, 100% data coverage
